01 · ABOUT

Not an app.
A living
network.

Most digital products connect information, content, or messages. EchoCity is designed around continuous life.

Yesterday remains connected to today. Relationships gain history. Choices create consequences. Ordinary people leave memories.

它不是一次性体验，也不是普通聊天工具。它是一座会记住居民、关系和城市历史的数字城市。

03 · CIVILIZATION VALUES

Different cultures. Shared life.

EchoCity does not ask everyone to become the same. It asks whether people with different backgrounds can live together with dignity and responsibility.

Dignity

Every resident is treated as a person, not a data point.

Responsibility

Choices matter and become part of the shared story.

Dialogue

Differences are addressed through honest conversation.

Mutual Growth

Residents and the city develop together over time.
